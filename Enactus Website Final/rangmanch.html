Rangmanch

A social initiative by Enactus DTU aims at empowering artists practicing artforms that have seen an exponential decline in the past decade or so.

About Rangmanch

Reviving lost artforms , one at a time.

Enactus DTU’s latest venture Project Rangmanch is an initiative to preserve the legacy of the torch bearers of our heritage. It provides assistance to several artists to adapt their art-form in accordance to the modern day scenario and bring about a transition of these artists from the informal to the formal business environment. Rangmanch has a decentralised system with several micro enterprises collaborating together. This project acts as a locus for all such buried artforms and promotes culture sharing in the society. By creating a multi sided business platform, we intend to eradicate the role of middlemen. 

Problem

People isolating themselves from the old indian traditions.

The past decade, rapid urbanization and industrialization has led to the adven and ferocious increase in modern technology. An era where the digital prevails the ethnic and the ones affected profoundly are the ones who actually practiced these ethinc satires. Devoid of their limelight and market, the victim has secluded and derailed itself into a shallow and dark world, far far from reality and itno oblivion.

Our Work

Providing a 'mauka' to these artists to help them revive the traditions.

Project Rangmanch aims at finding these curative and highly crafted individuals and channels ways and businnes models that are well adapted to this present day world to help them not only merely thrive but also provide them with a stage, a "Manch", per say where they can showcase their art and grapple their lost stardom and way of life.

Future Goals

To revive a legacy.

  1. Impacting greater target groups with revised ideas and scripts.
  2. Search for new artform and pertaining to their needs
  3. Product based venture
