Project Unnati

Project Unnati aims at uprooting digital illiteracy from urban slums of Delhi NCR by opening centers of digital excellence, the 'Unnati Cafes'.

ABOUT UNNATI

Project Unnati aims at reducing the plights of disadvantaged group of people living in urban slums of Delhi NCR through information sharing by imparting digital and financial literacy, thus bridging the ga p between theory, policy and practice. Under Project Unnati multipurpose Unnati Cafes are set up in rural and sub-urban areas which implement our courses and programs, independently maintained by our entrepreneurs.

THE PROBLEM

Over the last decade, the number of internet users in our country has skyrocketed, making us the second largest internet user in the world. In spite of that, we still live in a society where the implementation of digital literacy at grassroot level is far from adequate. We believe in the dictum ‘Knowledge is Power’ and aim to use this power to transform lives of urban slumdwellers.This is where PROJECT UNNATI comes in.

WHAT ARE WE DOING

We create Unnati Entrepreneurs who manage our educational hubs, the 'Unnati Cafes'. These cafes function as nodal agencies which implement our courses, strategies and programs. We aim at utterly digitising the learning process with the introduction of Unnati Software.

FUTURE GOALS

  1. Disseminating literacy through digital means to promote learning via digital media.
  2. Enhancing employment opportunities for the youth in our established cafés.
  3. A collaboration with the government on various policies will help us widen the reach of Project Unnati.
